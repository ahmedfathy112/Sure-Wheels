Lamborghini Aventador

  1. Model: Lamborghini Aventador
  2. Engine: 6.5-liter V12
  3. Horsepower: 770 hp
  4. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds
  5. Top speed: 350 km/h
  6. Drive system: All-wheel drive
  7. Price: Approximately 15,600,000 EGP

Lamborghini Countach

  1. Model: Lamborghini Countach
  2. Production: 1974-1990
  3. Engine: 4.0-5.2-liter V12
  4. Horsepower: 375-455 hp
  5. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 5.7-4.8 seconds
  6. Top speed: 309-295 km/h
  7. Drive system: Rear-wheel drive (early models), all-wheel drive (later models)
  8. Price: The current cost of buying a Lamborghini Countach at auctions is hundreds of thousands of US dollars.

Lamborghini Diablo

  1. Model: Lamborghini Diablo
  2. Production: 1990-2001
  3. Engine: 5.7-6.0-liter V12
  4. Horsepower: 485-575 hp
  5. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 4.5-3.9 seconds
  6. Top speed: 325-341 km/h
  7. Drive system: Rear-wheel drive (early models), all-wheel drive (later models)
  8. Price: The car is now considered used and the price varies depending on the condition and location of the car.

Lamborghini Gallardo

  1. Model: Lamborghini Gallardo
  2. Production: 2003-2013
  3. Engine: 5.0-5.2-liter V10
  4. Horsepower: 500-570 hp
  5. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 4.2-3.4 seconds
  6. Top speed: 309-325 km/h
  7. Drive system: Rear-wheel drive (early models), all-wheel drive (later models)
  8. Price: The cost of buying a new Lamborghini Gallardo ranges between $180,000-$240,000 USD.

Lamborghini Murcielago

  1. Model: Lamborghini Murcielago
  2. Production: 2001-2010
  3. Engine: 6.2-6.5-liter V12
  4. Horsepower: 572-661 hp
  5. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 3.4-3.8 seconds
  6. Top speed: 330-345 km/h
  7. Drive system: All-wheel drive
  8. Price: The cost of buying a new Lamborghini Murcielago is around $354,000 USD.

Lamborghini Reventon

  1. Model: Lamborghini Reventon
  2. Production: 2008-2009
  3. Engine: 6.5-liter V12
  4. Horsepower: 650 hp
  5. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 3.4 seconds
  6. Top speed: 340 km/h
  7. Drive system: All-wheel drive
  8. Price: Only 20 units of this car were produced with a price tag exceeding $1.6 million USD.

Lamborghini Sesto Elemento

  1. Model: Lamborghini Sesto Elemento
  2. Production: 2011-2012 (only 20 units produced)
  3. Engine: 5.2-liter V10
  4. Horsepower: 570 hp
  5. Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 2.5 seconds
  6. Top speed: 350 km/h
  7. Drive system: All-wheel drive
  8. Price: All units of this car were sold at a price exceeding $2.2 million USD.
